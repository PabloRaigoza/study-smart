About

Study Smart is a web application developed by Bodong Liu and Pablo Raigoza, two undergrads at Cornell University. You can record your work sessions tasks, record the time you spend on them, and view your progress later. Study Smart is designed to help you stay organized and focused on your goals. Note that the website is still under construction and some features may not work as expected. Future updates: - A working calendar - A visualized progress tracker - Smart suggestions for scheduling tasks - And more!

Made by Bodong Liu and Pablo Raigoza
